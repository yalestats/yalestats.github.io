You are a financial analyst working for a hedge fund. You have been given a dataset containing the daily opening and closing stock prices of Apple from March 2010 - February 2020. Your task is to determine if there is a significant relationship between trading volume and daily changes in stock price.

The dataset contains the following variables for each day:

\(Close\) - the daily closing price of Apple stock in USD

\(Open\) - the daily opening price of Apple stock in USD
